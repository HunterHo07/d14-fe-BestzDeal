Competitive Landscape

How BestzDeal compares to traditional marketplaces

Amazon

Est. 1994
Seller-focused marketplace

Pros

  • Vast inventory
  • Fast shipping
  • Established brand

Cons

  • Favors large sellers
  • Overwhelming options
  • Impersonal experience
Fee Structure: 8-15% commission + $39.99/mo

eBay

Est. 1995
Auction & fixed-price marketplace

Pros

  • Auction format
  • Wide variety
  • Established platform

Cons

  • Declining relevance
  • Seller-initiated only
  • Limited local focus
Fee Structure: 10-12% commission + listing fees

Etsy

Est. 2005
Handmade & vintage marketplace

Pros

  • Niche focus
  • Creative products
  • Community feel

Cons

  • Limited to specific categories
  • Seller-initiated only
  • Higher prices
Fee Structure: 6.5% commission + $0.20/listing

BestzDeal

Est. 2023
Reverse marketplace

Pros

  • Buyer-initiated
  • Competitive offers
  • Local seller focus
  • Time-saving

Cons

  • Newer platform
  • Building seller network
  • Limited categories (initially)
Fee Structure: 5% commission, optional $19.99/mo premium
